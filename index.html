About

Research-oriented Computer Scientist with a strong foundation in artificial intelligence, deep learning, computer vision and representation learning — experienced in building novel architectures, interpretable AI systems, and vision-based analytical models across healthcare and real-world domains.

My research interests span computer vision, scene understanding, representation learning, weakly supervised learning, explainable artificial intelligence (XAI), and intelligent perception systems for complex environments. I am particularly interested in developing robust, interpretable, and data-efficient AI methodologies that can address challenging real-world problems under limited supervision, uncertainty, and domain variability. My recent research has focused on medical image analysis, explainable AI evaluation, graph neural networks, multimodal learning, and deep representation learning, with experience designing novel neural architectures, conducting rigorous experimental evaluations, ablation studies, and reproducible AI research. I have worked extensively on developing interpretable and reliable deep learning systems for healthcare diagnostics, including retinal disease analysis, dermatological image classification, neonatal brain imaging, and clinical decision support applications. Beyond healthcare AI, I am deeply interested in applying computer vision and intelligent perception systems to interdisciplinary domains such as environmental monitoring, biodiversity conservation, precision agriculture, and autonomous systems. My broader research vision is to develop next-generation AI systems that can perceive, reason, explain, and adapt in complex real-world environments by integrating advances from computer vision, machine learning, robotics, causal reasoning, and scientific modeling. I am particularly motivated to pursue a PhD that enables interdisciplinary research at the intersection of artificial intelligence, computer vision, robotics, environmental science, and intelligent autonomous systems, where I can contribute to the development of trustworthy, explainable, and socially impactful AI technologies.
